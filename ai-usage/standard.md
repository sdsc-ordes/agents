# AI Usage Policy

## Definitions

* **AI Code** has been generated and underwent light or no human review. aka Vibe Code.
* **Human Code** is owned by a human. This implies the owner has edited and fully understood (not only read) it. They are responsible for it, regardless of how it was originally produced.

## Rules

This project has strict rules for AI usage in contributions:

* Authors MAY include agent guidelines. Such artifacts should be committed in the `.agents/` directory.
* Contributions MUST be Human Code. Only contribute to this project if you can explain your changes at the level of the API boundary you are calling (function inputs, outputs and behavior) without the aid of AI tools.
* Authors MUST own issues and comments. This means that any content generated with AI assistance must have been reviewed and edited by a human who takes responsibility before submission.
* Authors MAY use AI reviews: Use of agentic AI for reviews is encouraged before requesting a human review.

## There are Humans Here

Please remember that {project} is maintained by humans.

Every discussion, issue, and pull request description is reviewed by humans before publishing. It is a boundary point at which people interact. Don't put the burden of validation on the maintainer. Ensure your contributions are scoped and concise.

## AI is Welcome Here

Our reason for the strict AI policy is not an anti-AI stance, but to keep oversight and quality control over the project.

We include this section to be transparent about the project's use of AI for people who may disagree with it, and to address the misconception that this policy is anti-AI in nature.
